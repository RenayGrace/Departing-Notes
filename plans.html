The Keychain · $25

The Keychain

The keychain is a physical QR‑code tag that first responders can scan in an emergency. When scanned, your emergency contacts are notified, your emergency vault opens according to the platform's rules, and your designated letters can be triggered for release. This is a unique signature feature that no competitor currently offers.

$25 — unless included in a paid plan.
Ethical Release Rules

The vault stays sealed

Your vault stays sealed until death is confirmed by documentation, such as a death certificate. This rule is non‑negotiable and is set by the platform, not by the member. After death is confirmed, the member's chosen timing rules control when materials are released. Release can be immediate or delayed for up to one year.

Protected by the platform — never opened early.
Milestone Delivery

Milestone Delivery

Members can write letters to be held and delivered at future life moments, such as a child's graduation, wedding, birthday, adoption, major achievement, or other meaningful milestone. These letters are entrusted to a designated distributor, who decides the right moment to deliver them.

A presence that lasts across a whole life.
