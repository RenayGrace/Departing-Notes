CCPA Notice

California Consumer Privacy Act · Last updated June 3, 2026

Categories of personal information we collect

  • Identifiers: name, email address, date of birth, state of residence.
  • Account & vault content you choose to store.
  • Commercial information: membership tier and billing history.
  • Internet activity: limited, anonymized analytics.

Purpose of collection

We collect this information solely to operate your account, secure your vault, deliver your designated messages upon a verified passing, and improve the service.

Right to opt out of sale

DepartingNotes does not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of, because we never sell your data — to anyone, for any reason.

Your rights

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to non‑discrimination for exercising your rights.

How to submit a request

Email privacy@departingnotes.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." We respond within 45 days.
